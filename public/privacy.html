Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

Welcome to Extractify. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website and in using our products and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of personal information we collect include:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and billing details when you sign up.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our service, such as the pages you visit, the features you use, and the time and date of your visits.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect from all of our services to provide, maintain, protect and improve them, to develop new ones, and to protect Extractify and our users. We also use this information to offer you tailored content.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share personal information with companies, organizations, and individuals outside of Extractify unless one of the following circumstances applies:

  • With your consent.
  • For legal reasons, such as to meet any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request.

4. Data Security

We work hard to protect Extractify and our users from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information we hold. In particular:

  • We encrypt many of our services using SSL.
  • We review our information collection, storage, and processing practices, including physical security measures.

5. Your Rights

You may update or correct your account information and email preferences at any time by logging into your account settings page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@extractify.com.
