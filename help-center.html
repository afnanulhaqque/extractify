Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our platform.

How do I start my first extraction? ▼
Simply enter the URL of the website you want to scrape into the dashboard, select the data points you want to capture using our visual selector, and hit "Run". You'll have your data in seconds.
Does Extractify handle JavaScript-heavy websites? ▼
Yes! Our advanced cloud-based browser engine can render JavaScript, handle single-page applications (SPAs), and wait for dynamic content to load before extraction.
Can I schedule scrapers to run daily? ▼
Absolutely. With our Automation features, you can schedule any project to run at specific intervals (hourly, daily, weekly) and receive the data automatically.
What file formats are supported for export? ▼
We support XLSX (Excel), CSV, and JSON formats for all data extractions. You can also sync data directly to Google Sheets or via Webhooks.
